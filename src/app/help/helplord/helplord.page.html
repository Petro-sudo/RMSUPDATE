What is TRES

Based on the estimates of the magnitude of the shortage of student accommodation ,we developed T_Res .T_Res is a digital platform started in January 2020 with the intention of enhancing the process of applying for student accommodation and Registration of the prospective residential Complexes by the owners. In keeping with the technological changes, we have opted to provide our services on the digital platforms to enable our clients to access our services at any time to their convenience. Students can apply for their accommodation and receive frequent feedback on the status of their application without the need to visit a physical office. The same functionality is available for the residential companies to register their companies online.
