Who we Are


TRes is a digital platform built to enhance the quality of life for students in student accommodation while providing appropriate tools and support to accommodation providers. The design and functionality of the platform aims to create ongoing interaction between students, accommodation providers and University administrators in a spirit of cooperation and mutual respect. Each of the participants must always feel like their voice is heard.


The core idea behind TRes is that student accommodation is not only big business, it is more importantly about human dignity. It is about the totality of the experience of being a university student. It is one of the critical contributory factors to student performance.


Every aspect of TRes reflects this core idea. From students searching for accommodation through to their daily lived experience; and from accommodation providers applying for accreditation or tendering a lease through to the daily management of the accommodation facility, TRes makes interactions seamless, experiences memorable and results pleasing


In essence, TRes is the one stop shop for student accommodation and soon to be one stop shop for student living.
